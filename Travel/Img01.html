PARIS, FRANCE


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Love" and a global hub for art, fashion, and culture. It is home to iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. The city's charming streets, world-class cuisine, and vibrant atmosphere make it a must-visit destination. Visitors can explore the Champs-Élysées, enjoy a Seine River cruise, or indulge in famous French pastries at local cafés. Paris is also renowned for its rich history, with historical sites like the Palace of Versailles and the artistic district of Montmartre.
