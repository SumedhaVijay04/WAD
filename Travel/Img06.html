JAIPUR, INDIA


Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known as the "Pink City" due to its distinctive pink-colored buildings. It is a major cultural and historical hub in India, offering stunning palaces, forts, and vibrant markets. The majestic Amer Fort, the grand City Palace, and the iconic Hawa Mahal are some of its top attractions. Jaipur is also famous for its traditional handicrafts, bustling bazaars, and delicious Rajasthani cuisine. The city's blend of royal heritage, colorful culture, and warm hospitality makes it a must-visit destination in India.
