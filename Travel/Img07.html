GOA, INDIA


Goa, India's smallest state, is a tropical paradise known for its golden beaches, lively nightlife, and Portuguese heritage. It attracts travelers with its scenic coastline, beachside shacks, and adventure sports like parasailing and jet skiing. The state also boasts historic sites such as the Basilica of Bom Jesus and Aguada Fort. Visitors can explore charming villages, enjoy vibrant festivals, or relax in luxury resorts.
