ROME, ITALY


Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city steeped in history and home to some of the world's most famous ancient landmarks. The Colosseum, the Pantheon, and the Roman Forum offer a glimpse into the grandeur of the Roman Empire. Vatican City, an independent city-state within Rome, houses St. Peter's Basilica, the Sistine Chapel, and the Vatican Museums. Visitors can also enjoy authentic Italian cuisine, from pasta to gelato, while exploring the charming streets of Trastevere or tossing a coin into the Trevi Fountain for good luck.
